Typography

Web typography refers to the appearance of all the text on your website. It includes basic CSS text properties like what font to use and whether it should be italic or not, but typography is much more than that. It's about the space between and around letters, words, and lines. It is the size of the different text sequences in relation to each other and the story behind each font family
